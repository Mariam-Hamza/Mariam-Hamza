The first novel in the "DCI Anna Tate Thriller" series , titled "The Accused" , was published in 2021, while the third novel titled "Litle Boy Lost", was published in 2023 by the author J.B. Carter, who has received some awards for her previous novel, including the best crime novel award from The Crime Writers Association magazine.

Main Characters:


DCI Anna Tate: A talented but troubled police detective struggling with her past.

DI Anna Blake: Anna’s partner, a young and ambitious detective.

DS Jason Chen: An officer on Anna’s team loyal and intelligent.



Events:


"In Safe Hands": The story begins with the discovery of the body of a young girl in the woods. Anna and her team are tasked with investigating the case, but they soon realize they are facing a clever and elusive killer.
"At the Threshold of Darkness": While Anna is still dealing with the shock of the previous case, she is called to investigate a brutal murder of a wealthy businessman. She quickly discovers that the case is connected to an old case she thought was solved long ago.
"The Lost Little Boy": When a young boy goes missing from his family home, Anna and her team are tasked with finding him. But as time passes, it becomes clear that the boy is not only in danger but is also the key to solving a dark mystery.
