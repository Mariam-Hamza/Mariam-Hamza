Space Forces

A Critical History of Life in Outer Space

by Fred Scharmen

Biography:
Fred Scharmen is the co-founder of the Working Group on Adaptive Systems, an art and design consultancy based in Baltimore, Maryland. His work as a designer and researcher is about how we imagine new spaces for future worlds, and about who is invited into them. He received his Masters in Architecture from Yale University, and Bachelors Degree in Architecture from the University of Maryland, College Park. In 2022, he co-founded Brick Moon, a consultancy for space habitat design. Scharmen's first book, Space Settlements, was published in 2019 by Columbia University Press. His second book, Space Forces, was published in 2021 by Verso. His work has been exhibited internationally, at the Venice Architecture Biennale, and the Museum of the Future in Dubai. His writing has been published in the Journal of Architectural Education, Log, CLOG, Volume, and Domus.
